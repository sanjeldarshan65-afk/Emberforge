Emberforge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version

Emberforge collects nothing. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, no trackers, and no advertising.

Where thy data lives

Everything you enter — workouts, personal records, body measurements, nutrition logs, routines, and settings — is stored only on your own device, in your browser's (or app's) local storage. It is never transmitted anywhere. Deleting the app, clearing site data, or using "Sever thy save" in Settings permanently erases it.

Backups

The optional "Preserve thy save" feature exports your data as a file to your device. That file goes wherever you choose to put it; Emberforge never sees it again unless you import it back.

Third parties

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts when online. No user data is sent with those requests beyond what any web request includes. No other third-party services are used.

Health disclaimer

Emberforge is a logbook, not a medical device. Training and nutrition figures it produces are generic estimates — consult a qualified professional before making health decisions.

Contact

Questions may be sent to the developer through the app's store listing.
