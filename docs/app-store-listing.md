## Description (4000 char limit)

```
Thy body is thy covenant. Tend the flame.

Emberforge turns your training log into a dark-fantasy RPG. Every set you lift is a battle. Every workout earns souls and XP. Every week you show up feeds a streak that the game — and your body — remembers.

This isn't gamification bolted onto a spreadsheet. It's a full progression system built around your real training data:

• BATTLES — log any lift, chase your estimated 1RM, and watch PRs trigger loot drops
• RANKS — climb a named ladder from Unkindled to Titan of the Forge as your level rises
• TRIALS — covenants sworn against real goals: PRs, streaks, macros, the golden taper
• THE CONSTELLATION — spend souls on a skill tree of permanent training buffs
• THE HOARD — collect relics, trophies, and titles earned through play, not purchase
• THE RITE OF ASCENSION — prestige for veterans: reset for a permanent power bonus
• THE FIRE KEEPER — a coach that reads your training data and tells you what's lagging
• RATIONS — a macro tracker themed as feeding the vessel, with a meal-builder Cauldron
• RITE OF THE SEASON — a quarterly trial with a countdown and its own reward
• Streaks, curses, and buffs that respond to whether you actually showed up

No account required. No ads. No data leaves your device unless you turn on optional encrypted cloud sync yourself. Your training log — battles, PRs, body measurements — stays private and offline-first, always exportable, never sold.

Built for lifters who also love games, and for anyone who needs a reason to walk back into the gym tomorrow. The bar does not negotiate. Neither does the flame.

Forge thy legend.
```
(1,618 characters)

### Notes for submission
- Screenshots should lead with the Sanctum (Home) status bar — level, streak, souls — and the new Trials / Rite of the Season hero cards, since that's the first-run hook.
- App Store category: Health & Fitness (primary), Games (secondary, if Apple's listing allows a secondary category for this app type).
- Age rating: 4+ (no objectionable content; the dark-fantasy theme is stylistic, not graphic).
- Privacy: reiterate in the App Privacy questionnaire that no data is collected by default (local-only storage), and that optional cloud sync is user-supplied and encrypted — see `Settings → cloudEndpoint`.
